Dovac

Type

Individual project

Realisation

Graphic design
Model prototyping

Date

January-March, 2022

Dovac is an application for the dematerialization of the files of temporary workers at the University of Franche-Comté. It is used to allow professionals to complete an administrative file in order to be able to intervene at the University as a temporary worker. On the administration side, the application allows the resource persons to manage all the files of temporary workers and to complete the hours they carry out.

This project was divided into two parts. The first part was carried out in groups of three. It consisted in analyzing Dovac's competition, and therefore doing a market study. Then, we had to create three typical personas, three imaginary people who would need to use Dovac. So we imagined two temporary workers and an administrator.

In addition, during this part we made several experience maps, listed the features and finally, we designed a moodboard related to the application.

Concerning the second part, it was done individually. During this time, we each developed a graphic model taking into account the user's path. The graphic model as well as the prototyping were realized with figma, a vector graphics editor and prototyping tool.
