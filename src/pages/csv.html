Realisation

Date

September-January, 2022

The association Coeuvatte-Suarcine-Vendeline, CSV, is an association which was created in 2005 for the discovery, the information, the restoration and the safeguard of the Heritage of the villages located in the south of the territory of Belfort. Its head office is located in Delle. The idea of the project was born following a request of the association which wished to change its obsolete website. The objective was to completely redo the association's website in order to attract young people and to develop on the Internet.

In this project, I was in charge of design. Indeed, I realized the graphic model of the site of the association by taking into account the criteria that our sponsors gave us, as well as the graphic charter of this one. We used colors such as green and brown which reminded us of the heritage, and blue, which refers to the name Coeuvatte-Suarcine-Vendeline, the name of the rivers which surround Delle.

I also helped my group on the communication part. We had to set up a manual with all the necessary indications to change the elements of the association's website on Wordpress, in order to allow a better facility to our sponsors to modify and update their website.

This project allowed us to have experience with outside clients who have requirements and expectations for their project. It wasn't always easy, sometimes we encountered problems, but this project gave me experience for the future in the professional world.
