Norma Ludus

Type

Collective project

Realisation

Graphic design
Web integration

Date

February-June, 2021

Norma Ludus is an application that allows you to list all the official rules of your favorite board games, as well as a wide variety of alternative rules, each one more fun than the other, so you'll never argue or get bored of your games again. Not enough? You also have the possibility to buy or sell games.

This project was born from an innovative idea of our group. First, we created a landing page for the application. On this page, the user can register or login and discover more about Norma Ludus. Concerning the name of the project, we chose "Norma Ludus" because it combines "Norma" which means "rules" in Latin and "Ludus" which means "game". It's a name that, despite its Latin sound, remains simple to pronounce and can stay in your head thanks to its funny pronunciation.

Clear design, the theme used in the project being board games, the atmosphere created refers to conviviality, entertainment and fun. The platform benefits from a simple, airy and uncluttered space, quite minimalist.

During this project, we had to carry out two distinct phases. The first one was based on research and analysis. The second phase consisted of creating the graphic model and the final prototype using the Adobe XD application.

Finally, we integrated the graphic model in responsive HTML/CSS/JS using PhpStorm.
